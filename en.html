OUR STORY

80 years of Busan's noodles, made by your child's own hands

Gupo noodles are a beloved Busan comfort food, dried in the salty river breeze of the Nakdong River for a uniquely chewy texture. At the Gupo Noodle Experience Center, children knead the dough, turn the noodle press, and watch fresh noodles come to life.

Afterward, everyone takes home a meal kit of the noodles, sujebi (hand-torn dough), and broth they made — so the memory continues at your dinner table.

FAQ

What ages can join?

Recommended for ages 3 (36 months) and up. Younger children can join with a parent's help; an accompanying adult is free.

How do we book?

Reserve in advance through Naver (foreign-visitor reservation supported). For assistance, call +82-10-2608-5099. Individual sessions run mainly on weekends and public holidays.

What do we take home?

A meal kit of the noodles, sujebi (hand-torn dough) and coin-broth your child made — ready to cook at home.

Is parking available?

There is no private parking lot. Public transit is recommended (3-min walk from Gupo Station). Nearby paid lots are available.

What if we are late?

Entry is allowed up to 15 minutes after the session start. Please call ahead. Changes to another session and refunds are not available for late arrivals.
